Daily Facts

Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren’t.

Botanically, bananas fit the definition of berries, while strawberries do not!

Honey never spoils.

Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.

Wombat poop is cube-shaped.

Wombats produce cube-shaped poop, which helps keep it from rolling away and marks their territory.

Sharks existed before trees.

Sharks have been around for over 400 million years, while trees evolved around 350 million years ago.

Octopuses have three hearts.

Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. Interestingly, the heart that delivers blood to the body stops when the octopus swims, which is why they prefer crawling to swimming.

Cleopatra lived closer in time to the moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid.

Cleopatra’s reign in Egypt was around 2,000 years after the Great Pyramid of Giza was built, and only about 2,000 years before the first moon landing.

Sloths can hold their breath longer than dolphins.

Sloths can hold their breath for up to 40 minutes, while dolphins can only manage around 10 minutes.

The shortest war in history lasted only 38 to 45 minutes..

The Anglo-Zanzibar War took place between the United Kingdom and the Sultanate of Zanzibar on August 27, 1896.

Pineapples were once so rare that they were rented out as status symbols.

In the 18th century, pineapples were extremely expensive in Europe, and wealthy people would rent them for parties to show off their wealth.

Busted

We only use 10% of our brains.

Busted:This is a common myth. In reality, we use nearly all parts of our brain, even when we're at rest. Brain imaging shows activity in every part of the brain, even while sleeping.

Busted:The Great Wall is not visible to the naked eye from space without aid. It's too narrow and blends in with the natural surroundings.

Busted:Goldfish actually have a memory span of several months and can be trained to respond to cues and navigate mazes.
